Typhoid Diagnosis
Correct Diagnosis For Your Symptoms
Symptoms Below
  • High Fever
  • Dull Frontal Headache
  • Anorexia: Eating disorder and weight loss
  • Diarrhea
  • Constipation
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Chills
  • Cough
  • Malaise or fatigue
Treatment Options
Primary Options

Ciprofloxacin: Children - Consult a specialist for guidiance on dose;
Adults - 500mg orally twice daily

OR

Ofloxacin: Children - Consult a specialist for guidiance on dose;
Adults - 400mg orally twice daily

Secordary Options

Chloramphenicol: Children and Adults - 50-100mg/kg/day orally/intravenously given in divided dosed every 6 hours
