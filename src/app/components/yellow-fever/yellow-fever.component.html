Yellow Fever Diagnosis
Correct Diagnosis For Your Symptoms
Symptoms Below
  • Fever
  • Conjunctiva injection or Red eyes
  • Relative bradycardia or low heart beat
  • Jaundice or yellowish Skin
  • Hemorrhagic diathesis or Bleeding
  • Hypotension or Low blood Pressure
  • Lethargy or Laziness or Fatigue
  • Vomiting
Treatment Options

Ribavirin: 33 mg/kg intravenously as a loading dose, followed by 16 mg/kg every 6 hours for 4 days, then 8 mg/kg every 8 hours for 6 days

Maintaining good nutrition and taking antibiotics
